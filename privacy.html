Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Subtitle Sanctuary, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address when you create an account or subscribe.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and general interaction patterns.
  • Uploaded content: Audio, video, and text files you upload for alignment processing.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by our payment provider (LemonSqueezy / Paddle). We do not store credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the alignment service.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To communicate service updates and respond to support requests.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve product quality.

3. Data Processing & Retention

Uploaded audio, video, and text files are processed ephemerally. Files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of processing completion. We do not store, cache, or use your uploaded content for any purpose beyond providing the alignment service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Payment processors (LemonSqueezy / Paddle) for transaction processing.
  • Hosting providers (Vercel) for service delivery.
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights.

5. Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies for authentication and session management. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@subtitlesanctuary.com.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure file handling, and access controls to protect your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact: hello@subtitlesanctuary.com
